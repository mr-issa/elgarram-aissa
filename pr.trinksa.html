Turkish Elegance: Scaling Women’s Leather Bags from Qatar & Oman into Saudi Arabia

A.EL GARRAM & AEM.TU.tr • YEARs 2023 – 2024
Scroll

Project Overvie

Launched a performance-driven digital campaign for a Turkish brand selling premium women’s leather bags (199–399 SAR) across the Gulf. After proven success in Qatar and Oman (3.8% conversion, 4.2x ROAS), expanded into Saudi Arabia using Meta Ads (60%), TikTok Spark Ads (25%), and Google PMax (15%).

Built Arabic-compliant creatives with local influencers

Optimized via GA4, Looker Studio, and real-time A/B testing

Achieved CAC of 21 SAR and 5.1% conversion rate

399 SAR Gift Set drove 48% of KSA revenue during Ramadan/Eid

Full GA4 + Meta Pixel + TikTok Pixel integration with last-non-direct click attribution Built real-time Looker Studio dashboard tracking: ROAS, CAC, AOV, LTV Reduced CAC from 38 SAR → 21 SAR by excluding low-intent cities (e.g., Jeddah “free bag” searchers) Weekly optimization loop: Pause underperforming creatives within 48 hours
