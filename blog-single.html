Performance Journal: Ouarzazate Hotel Digital Marketing & Tourism Strategy

By MDG.LAB & A.EL GARRAM • March, 2024
Scroll

Project Overvie

This project showcases a complete digital marketing and hospitality management strategy developed for a hotel in Ouarzazate, the cinematic and touristic capital of Morocco. The goal was to design and execute a data-driven marketing campaign that enhances the hotel’s online visibility, guest engagement, and direct bookings through an integrated digital ecosystem.

The initiative began with a comprehensive market and audience analysis focusing on local tourism trends, traveler behavior, and digital competition. Based on the insights, a holistic strategy was developed to position the hotel as a preferred destination for both national and international visitors exploring the Sahara, film studios, and desert landscapes.

Social Media Creation & Management Developed and branded official hotel pages on Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok. Implemented a content calendar featuring local attractions, guest experiences, and behind-the-scenes hospitality stories. Launched paid campaigns via Meta Ads Manager targeting adventure travelers, filmmakers, and eco-tourists.

Google Ads & Search Strategy Created search and display ad campaigns focused on High keywords In The region . Optimized ads using Google Analytics and Conversion Tracking to measure ROI and identify high-performing campaigns.

Website Management & SEO Optimization Redesigned and managed the hotel’s WordPress-based website, ensuring responsive design, clear CTAs, and direct booking functionality. Conducted on-page SEO (meta descriptions, headings, internal linking) and off-page SEO (Google My Business, backlinks) to boost organic ranking. Analytics & Performance Evaluation Used Google Analytics 4, Meta Insights, and Search Console to monitor performance indicators such as CTR, CPC, bounce rate, and booking conversions. Created detailed monthly analytical reports visualizing campaign progress and ROI improvement over time. Strategic Outcomes Increased the hotel’s website traffic by 65% and social media engagement by 120% within the first 3 months. Enhanced online reputation through TripAdvisor review optimization and influencer collaborations. Achieved a 30% boost in direct online bookings, reducing dependency on OTA commissions.
