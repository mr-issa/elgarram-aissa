Building a digital presence for a Moroccan textile cooperative — from local craft to global e-commerce success.

Starting from zero, I helped a small cooperative of carpet artisans in Ouarzazate create their first online store and achieve their first 10 sales in just one month. The process included designing an Etsy shop, launching Meta (Facebook & Instagram) and TikTok campaigns, optimizing visuals and ad content, and analyzing performance through Google Analytics. This project demonstrated how data-driven marketing and authentic storytelling can turn traditional craftsmanship into a growing online business.

Analytic Building a Digital Presence for a Moroccan Textile Cooperative — From Local Craft to Global E-Commerce Success

Starting from zero, I built their first online presence by launching a fully optimized Etsy shop, complete with SEO-rich listings, high-resolution product photography, and transparent shipping policies. Within one month, strategic multichannel campaigns across Meta (Facebook and Instagram) and TikTok—powered by authentic storytelling and data-driven targeting—drove 4,812 store visits, resulting in 10 confirmed sales and an average order value of $214.

Performance analytics revealed a 2.1% conversion rate (above Etsy’s benchmark), a customer acquisition cost of just $18.70, and an 11x return on ad spend. By blending cultural authenticity with real-time optimization—adjusting creatives, pausing low-ROAS ads, and refining mobile checkout—the project transformed traditional craftsmanship into a scalable e-commerce operation. This case proves that even micro-enterprises in remote regions can achieve global reach through disciplined digital strategy and narrative resonance.
